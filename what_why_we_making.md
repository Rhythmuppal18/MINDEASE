# MindMaze: What We Are Making & Why

This document explains the concept of MindMaze, its goals, and the psychological reasoning behind its interactive wellness mechanics.

---

## 1. What is MindMaze?
MindMaze is a gamified therapeutic wellness platform designed to help players reflect on, understand, and regulate common emotional struggles. Instead of presenting passive advice, the platform maps five cognitive-emotional states into interactive, 2D mini-game mechanics:

- **Anxiety (The Drift):** Guiding focus through a winding canyon using soft wind currents. Hasty, forceful inputs cause collision chaos.
- **Depression (The Grey City):** Restoring saturation and light to a dark, grey skyline by making matching card connections, representing gradual cognitive re-engagement.
- **Overthinking (The Thought Storm):** Connection-sorting drifting thought sequences into logical, structured chains before they scatter.
- **Stress (The Ocean of Stress):** Balancing a floating raft against erratic wave swells by maintaining rhythmic, calm corrections.
- **Impatience (The Chrono Trial):** Navigating a timed temple floor under Green Light / Red Light conditions. Demands freezing momentum completely during red phases, promoting impulse control.

---

## 2. Why Are We Making It?

### Gamifying Mindfulness
Traditional wellness apps often rely on text journals or voice-guided meditations, which can feel passive. MindMaze transforms mental health exercises into active, physical metaphors. By forcing the player to translate a mental state (like patience or steady focus) into coordinate movements, it builds active cognitive associations.

### Real-Time Neuro-Feedback Analogs
- **Anxiety Regulation:** Teaches that fighting tension with brute force makes control chaotic. Smooth, gentle adjustments are required.
- **Cognitive Reframing:** Teaches that untangling crowded thoughts or matching patterns is step-by-step work.
- **Impulse Control:** Teaches how to stop and breathe under timing pressure. The "safe zones" reinforce that having a stable, secure baseline is the key to managing urgency.

### Premium Accessibility
By building the application directly in the browser with highly polished styling, glassmorphism UI card components, detuned synthesizer soundscapes, and clean typography, it creates an elegant space that users want to return to daily.
